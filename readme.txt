Each time the game starts, the player is a different hero with a different ability (activated by 'E').

Pyro: Launches a fireball attack
Cryo: Launches an Ice attack
Hydro: Launches a bubble attack to all directions(up,down,left,right)
Dendro: Throws a boomerang that can be controlled with WASD

The hero may be changed with "H"

Block with the space bar (halves the damage taken).

Powerups:
Attack Speed - Increases your attack speed
Attack Power - Increases your attack power
Extra Heart - Increases your lives by one
Player Speed - Increases the hero's walking speed

Cheats:
ALT-R - Restart Room
ALT-G - Game restart
ALT-N - Go to next room(spooky)
ALT-D - Kill all enemies
ALT-F - Take all player lives away
ALT-E - Jump to end screen
ALT-H - Change hero
